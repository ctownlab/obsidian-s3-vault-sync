## Content
Since man is made in God's image yet is corrupted, Christ came to renew his image. [1]

Christ is necessary for this because man is made in His image. Man couldn't renew themselves because they are "in the image" rather than the image itself. Nor could angels because they are not in the image at all. [1]

This is like when a portrait is ruined, if the person in the portrait is there it can be repainted on the same canvas. [1]
