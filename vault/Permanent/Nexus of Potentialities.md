## Content
In the ancient world bodies were more thought of in terms of capabilities instead of atoms or physical parts. Fr. DeYoung coined the term "Nexus of Potentialities" for this. For example, eyes can refer to the ability to see rather than the parts in our heads. This helps in understanding how spiritual powers have influence.
