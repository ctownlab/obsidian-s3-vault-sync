## Content
Writing in our own words forces us take things out of the context of the thoughts in our head and put them in a new context. It's ok for notes on a new topic to be more extensive so long as they are being prepared for the slipbox. Notes on new topics tend to be more extensive. Zotero notes can be taking liberally as long as they end up in the slip box.
